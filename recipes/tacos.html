Tacos

Description

This is one of the recipes you can make anytime you want to fill your appetite.

Simple and easy.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon soy-based liquid seasoning
  • Â½ teaspoon chipotle powder
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Steps

  1. Combine ground pork, liquid seasoning, ground chipotle powder, and garlic in a bowl. Set aside for flavors to meld.
  2. Preheat a large skillet over high heat. Crumble ground pork into the hot skillet. Cook and stir until pork is completely browned and edges begin to crisp, 7 to 10 minutes. Drain any excess grease. Place pork in a bowl and keep warm. Wipe out skillet.
  3. Warm tortillas in skillet over medium-low heat, about 20 seconds per side. Divide meat mixture between tortillas and you're ready with the tacos.
